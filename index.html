// DATA ISOLATION

A major bottleneck of value chain Scope 3 reporting is contractor trust. We solve compliance data collection by placing robust, local-first guardrails between your operations and downstream networks.

SHIM operates as a secure, local-first data spacer. By running processing tasks entirely behind your local firewall, we eliminate standard enterprise IT database security audits and keep your sensitive cost structures completely private.

Protected Cost Metrics

Operational scripts process files locally inside your secure environment using WebAssembly (WASM). Your diesel rates, trade pricing, and margins remain strictly confidential. Only aggregated compliance indicators are exported.

Zero-Friction IT Footprint

Runs on existing hardware or a lightweight virtual machine. Deploys with zero inbound firewall rules, requiring no remote databases or open network endpoints. This bypasses typical IT security bottlenecks entirely.

Automated Audit Assurances

Our background file-watchers detect incoming documents (delivery dockets, telematics) and process them automatically. finalized outputs receive cryptographic proof marks, replacing weak estimates with auditable activity logs.
