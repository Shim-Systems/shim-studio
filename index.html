// SOLUTIONS

One Gatehouse Capture.
Dual Compliance Outputs.

Shifting site-gate capture from an administrative compliance overhead to an offensive, margin-protecting, and contract-securing asset.

Rather than chasing subcontractor dockets across disconnected databases, SHIM captures physical material dockets directly at the Point of Delivery. By processing delivery receipts locally, we automatically output verified compliance records for both Scope 3 Carbon and Circular Material Registers.

OUTPUT A // CARBON

ASRS Scope 3 General Ledger

Instantly matches on-site material delivery volumes with product-specific EPD registers. Generates verifiable Category 1 & 4 emission metrics, establishing audit-proof Scope 3 records.

Variables volume_m3, mix_class

Extracts exact material tonnages and recycled content percentages directly from dockets. Generates reporting outputs required to satisfy state-level ecologiQ and VIDA frameworks.

// THE EDGE

Navigating the physical realities of on-site compliance.

Software-only compliance platforms fail when they hit the physical, legal, and operational gates of active industrial sites.

Under AASB S2, carbon accounting carries the same legal weight as financial reporting. Just as financial audits reject guessed budgets, carbon audits legally require transaction-level data verified back to physical delivery dockets as unalterable "carbon transaction receipts."

Hardware-as-a-Service
(HaaS) Deployment.

True value-chain compliance demands a dedicated physical on-site checkpoint. We deliver the SHIM as a fully managed, turn-key on-premises hardware subscription—eliminating capital expenditure hurdles and ongoing support overheads.

Deploys on your LAN as a compact, silent, hardened hardware node requiring zero active local system administration. Zero-Retention Extraction

Operational files are uploaded securely for transient, zero-retention cloud-AI extraction. Structured environmental variables are written directly to your local database, and the raw files are immediately deleted from cloud storage.
