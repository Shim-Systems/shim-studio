SOLUTION_01

Retail & FMCG Distribution

Major retail and logistics networks require regular, verified carbon metrics per delivery, but manual spreadsheets can't isolate freight carbon or packaging weights. We deploy local-first data shims to automatically track refrigerated transport fuel, geofenced loading-dock idle runtimes, and FMCG packaging weights directly from supplier manifests—keeping you pre-qualified without administrative gridlock.

Ingested Variables Transport fuel, PTO runtime, packaging manifest weights
Integration Model Fleet telematics APIs, commercial fuel logs, enterprise ERP manifests
SOLUTION_02

Civil Infrastructure & Capital Works

Builders managing new infrastructure assets, distribution hubs, or facility upgrades face critical administrative delays manually auditing materials. Our local parser shims ingest raw concrete batch delivery dockets and structural steel test certificates directly at the subcontractor level—extracting exact design strengths (MPa) and flyash/slag replacement percentages (SCM%) automatically.

Ingested Variables Volume (m³), MPa compressive class, SCM% cement, Steel Heat Numbers
Integration Model Logistics databases, fabrication software, standard EPD-API mapping
SOLUTION_03

Resources & Heavy Services

Quarrying, bulk mineral haulage, and civil contracting businesses servicing tier-1 mining operators must supply audit-ready carbon footprints to secure contract extensions. However, sharing raw machine logs risks exposing proprietary fuel pricing and operating margins. We parse heavy equipment machine telemetry locally behind your firewall, exporting only clean, compliance-grade factor ledgers.

Ingested Variables Machine duty cycles, fuel rate telemetry, J1939 HEX packets, haulage dockets
Integration Model Heavy equipment telemetry networks, local telemetry parsers

// DATA ISOLATION

A major bottleneck of Scope 3 reporting for retail and heavy supply chains is contractor trust. We solve compliance data collection by placing robust, local-first guardrails between your operations and downstream networks.

SHIM operates as a secure, local-first data spacer. By running processing tasks entirely behind your local firewall, we eliminate standard enterprise IT database security audits and keep your sensitive cost structures completely private.

Protected Cost Metrics

Operational scripts process files locally inside your secure environment using WebAssembly (WASM). Your diesel rates, trade pricing, and margins remain strictly confidential. Only aggregated compliance indicators are exported.

Zero-Friction IT Footprint

Runs on existing hardware or a lightweight virtual machine. Deploys with zero inbound firewall rules, requiring no remote databases or open network endpoints. This bypasses typical IT security bottlenecks entirely.

Automated Audit Assurances

Our background file-watchers detect incoming documents (delivery dockets, telematics) and process them automatically. finalized outputs receive cryptographic proof marks, replacing weak estimates with auditable activity logs.
