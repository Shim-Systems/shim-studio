## 🏛️ Target Client Profile: CASBE (Council Alliance for a Sustainable Built Environment)

### Relevant Attributes & Context
* **Jurisdiction & Reach:** An association operating under the Municipal Association of Victoria (MAV), representing over 35 Victorian councils (governing ~80% of the state's population).
* **Core Responsibilities:** Owns, manages, and updates the **BESS (Built Environment Sustainability Scorecard)** digital tools. They have newly expanded their ecosystem to mandate the **BESS Subdivisions module** across member councils.
* **Strategic Friction:** Actively trying to "improve compliance, standardize decision-making, and streamline sustainability expectations" while facing localized council resourcing and massive influxes of unstructured developer data.
* **Your Pitch Advantage:** You bypass all municipal network restrictions by running a **100% Air-Gapped Demonstration Model** on an upgraded **ASUS TUF A14 laptop** (RTX 5060 GPU / 32GB RAM). You pull the Wi-Fi plug and demonstrate immediate ROI with zero network risk.

## 🚀 The Three AI Project Alignment Strategies

### 📋 Project 1: The BESS Automation & Verification Engine
* **The Strategic Focus:** Data Strategy, Cross-Council Standardization, and Structural Compliance Audits.
* **Target Friction:** Local council planning departments are buried under hundreds of pages of messy, unstructured PDF attachments (site analysis plans, energy models, waste management sheets). Planners waste excessive manual hours verifying that the raw claims in these PDFs actually match the inputs submitted on the official BESS digital tool.
* **The Core Solution:** An entirely offline RAG pipeline (Ollama, LangChain, ChromaDB) that automatically ingests chaotic, multi-page application PDFs, cross-references them against CASBE framework checklists, and translates them into an interactive **JSON Compliance Dashboard** to highlight discrepancies instantly.

### 🤖 Project 2: The Multi-Agent ESD Advisory Copilot
* **The Strategic Focus:** Process Rewiring, Operating Leverage, and Hallucination-Free Legal Guardrails.
* **Target Friction:** CASBE's roving *Shared Service SSF (Sustainable Subdivisions Framework) Advisors* spend immense billable hours manually drafting custom "Request for Further Information" (RFI) briefs, advisory statements, and compliance summaries for time-poor regional and metro planning clerks.
* **The Core Solution:** A **3-agent orchestration system** (Policy Researcher, Drafting Writer, and Compliance Auditor) featuring an explicit **programmatic circuit-breaker**. The system automatically generates legally sound advisory briefs and RFI letters in seconds, but halts execution and alerts the human advisor if policy confidence drops below 90%.

### 🛡️ Project 3: The Secure AI Council Sandbox & Blueprint
* **The Strategic Focus:** Regional Change Management, Governance, and Data Sovereignty.
* **Target Friction:** Individual council planners across Victoria are secretly pasting unapproved commercial real estate blueprints, sensitive site plans, and municipal internal communications into public web tools like ChatGPT and Claude to write summaries—exposing councils to severe data breaches while IT departments lag on rolling out safe enterprise alternatives.
* **The Core Solution:** A standardized alliance-wide governance blueprint comprising an **AI Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)** tailored for local government, an AI Maturity Roadmap, and a **100% offline, browser-based Streamlit chat UI** hooked directly into local council infrastructure as a secure, drop-in replacement for public web tools.
