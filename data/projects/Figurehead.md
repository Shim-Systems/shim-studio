# Strategic Brief: Figurehead AI Alignment Strategy

## Client Profile & Key Attributes
* **Company:** Figurehead (Established 2007 | ~80 Employees)
* **Sector:** Mid-tier Commercial & Residential Construction Contractor
* **Core Drivers:** Long-term joint-venture relationships (e.g., Buxton Group), brand trust, quality execution, and strict risk mitigation.
* **Operational Footprint:** * **Heavy Administration:** 20+ Contract Administrators, Project Coordinators, and HSEQ Managers managing massive document backlogs.
    * **Growth Engine:** 4-person New Business/Bidding team.
    * **Innovation Structure:** Active **"NEXT:AI Group"** taskforce dedicated to exploring tech efficiency; single IT head resource.
* **Core Constraint:** Extreme sensitivity around data security, client NDAs, and proprietary project financials. Sending data to public cloud LLMs is a non-starter.

---

## AI Project Portfolio Options

### 1. The Local-First Compliance & Asset Ingestion Engine
* **Target Audience:** Contract Administrators, Project Coordinators, and HSEQ Managers.
* **Friction Addressed:** Hundreds of hours wasted manually auditing and cross-referencing unstructured subcontractor files, site logs, and legacy handover PDFs.
* **The Solution:** A local-first RAG pipeline using Ollama, LangChain, and ChromaDB that securely extracts unstructured text into a deterministic **JSON compliance dashboard**.
* **Strategic Value:** Massive operational time savings during project close-outs with **zero data leakage** since it runs 100% offline behind their firewall.

### 2. The Multi-Agent Autonomous Tender Proposal Engine
* **Target Audience:** Dylan Mercer (New Business Manager) and the 4-person bidding team.
* **Friction Addressed:** Sizable billable hours drained drafting custom Expressions of Interest (EOIs) and bids, paired with executive anxiety over AI "hallucinating" unsafe pricing or liability commitments.
* **The Solution:** A 3-agent orchestration system (Researcher, Writer, Auditor) utilizing a programmatic circuit-breaker that freezes execution if compliance confidence drops below **90%**.
* **Strategic Value:** Scales tender output volume by up to 60% safely, protecting historical profit margins while maximizing small team leverage.

### 3. The Shadow AI Enterprise Audit & Governance Blueprint
* **Target Audience:** Lou Raunik (Operations Manager), the NEXT:AI Group, and general staff.
* **Friction Addressed:** Management blindness to "Shadow AI," where employees secretly paste proprietary client briefs, contracts, or financials into public web tools like ChatGPT.
* **The Solution:** A formal AI Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) and Maturity Roadmap, paired with an offline, browser-based **Streamlit + Ollama chat UI** as a secure drop-in replacement.
* **Strategic Value:** Instantly sanitizes corporate liability and provides the NEXT:AI Group with a turnkey, risk-free foundational framework.
